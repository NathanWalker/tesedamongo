Hardware Overview

Teseda Diagnostic Systems utilize the Teseda Workbench™ (TWB) software with the Teseda 550 Diagnostic Hardware allowing engineers to work in a familiar device analysis context instead of in a sea of ones and zeros. Teseda Diagnostic Systems are designed for a variety of applications – from screening of potential field returns by remote customer quality engineers, to device stimulus and data capture for defect scanning, to defect isolation down to gate-level root-cause analysis.
The 550 Diagnostic Hardware is powerful enough to handle the most advanced device stimulus requirements, yet is easy to use, portable, air-cooled, and can quickly interface to other lab tools such as micro-probers, emission microscopes, and dynamic laser stimulus tools.
