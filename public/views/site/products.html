DI Lab

Comprehensive system.
Contains the Defect Isolator and Broken Chain Alayzer Tools.

  • ✔ 550 Diagnostic Hardware

    512 pins – 100MHz – 64MB per-pin pattern depth with flexible per-pin timing

  • ✔ Teseda WorkBench Software

    Operates interactively on the 550 Diagnostic System to provide failure information in terms of the design features where the failure occurred.

  • ✔ Defect Isolator (DI) Tool

    Maps scan test failures to the physical location on the die, reducing time to root-cause analysis, from weeks or months, to hours.

  • ✔ Broken Chain Analyzer (BCA) Tool

    Automatically pinpoints defective flip-flop locations in broken scan chains.
